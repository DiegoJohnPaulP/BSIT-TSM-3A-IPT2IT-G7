Falls

Canding Falls

The drive from Camiling, Tarlac to Canding Falls, San Clemente, Tarlac, which is northwest in its geographical location, has been the most picturesque I have ever seen in my province!!

The paved road became hilly and curvy with the beautiful Sierra Madre mountains at a distance. Had I not been driving, I would have taken more pictures of beautiful vistas without having to pull over.

Beach

Hundred Island

The Hundred Islands National Park is the first Philippine national park and a protected area located in Alaminos, Pangasinan.

Great day trip with lots of beautiful and quiet spots to chill. Make sure you book with a good boat / tour agency so that you can stop when you want to.

Baguio city

Baguio city

Baguio, perched high in the Cordillera Central Mountains, is a great place to escape the heat of the Philippine lowlands. Camp John Hay, originally built as a recreation area for U.S. soldiers, now is a full-fledged resort complete with a world-class golf course.

Definitely stop at the city market, where you can buy almost anything (just be prepared to haggle).
